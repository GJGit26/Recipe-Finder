Dosa Recipe

Crispy South Indian Crepes

Ingredients

For Dosa Batter:

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1/3 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 1/2 tsp fenugreek seeds (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (for soaking and grinding)

For Cooking:

  • Oil or ghee for greasing the pan
  • Chutney or sambar for serving

Instructions

  1. Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds for 4–6 hours.
  2. Grind to a smooth batter with enough water. The batter should be slightly thick and pourable.
  3. Cover and ferment overnight or 8–12 hours in a warm place until bubbly.
  4. Add salt and mix well before making dosas.
  5. Heat a non-stick or cast iron tawa. Pour a ladle of batter and spread it in a circular motion.
  6. Drizzle oil or ghee on the edges. Cook until golden and crisp. Fold and remove.
  7. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
