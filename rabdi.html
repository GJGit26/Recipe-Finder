Rabdi Recipe

A Traditional Indian Sweet Made with Thickened Milk

Ingredients

  • 1 liter full-fat milk
  • 1/4 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 10-12 almonds and pistachios (sliced)
  • 1/4 tsp cardamom powder
  • A few saffron strands (optional)
  • 1 tsp rose water or kewra water (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil milk in a heavy-bottomed pan. Lower the heat and simmer.
  2. Keep scraping the sides of the pan and stirring the milk to prevent burning.
  3. Continue cooking until the milk reduces to about 1/3 of its original volume.
  4. Add sugar and stir until dissolved.
  5. Add cardamom powder, saffron strands, and chopped nuts. Mix well.
  6. Cook for another 5 minutes, then turn off the heat.
  7. Add rose or kewra water if using. Let it cool completely.
  8. Serve chilled in small earthen pots or bowls, garnished with more nuts.
