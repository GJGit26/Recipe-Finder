Chole Bhature Recipe

A Popular North Indian Delight

Ingredients

For Chole (Chickpeas):

  • 1.5 cups chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 2 onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies, slit
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 2 tsp chole masala
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves
  • Oil for cooking

For Bhature (Fried Bread):

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp semolina (optional, for crispiness)
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed)
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

  1. Pressure cook soaked chickpeas with salt until soft. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil, add cumin seeds, then sauté onions until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies.
  3. Add tomato puree, cook until oil separates. Mix in turmeric, chole masala, and coriander powder.
  4. Add cooked chickpeas, mix well, and simmer for 10–15 minutes. Garnish with fresh coriander.
  5. For bhature, mix flour, semolina, salt, yogurt, and baking powder. Add water to knead a soft dough. Let it rest for 2 hours.
  6. Divide dough, roll into circles, and deep-fry in hot oil until puffed and golden.
  7. Serve hot chole with fluffy bhature, sliced onions, and lemon wedges.
