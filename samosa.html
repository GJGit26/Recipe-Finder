Samosa Recipe

Crispy & Spiced Indian Snack

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed)

For the Filling:

  • 2 boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1/2 cup boiled green peas
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1-2 chopped green chilies
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped coriander leaves
  • Oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Mix flour, salt, and oil in a bowl. Gradually add water to form a firm dough. Cover and rest for 30 minutes.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, green chilies, and then the mashed potatoes and peas.
  3. Add turmeric, coriander powder, garam masala, and salt. Mix well and cook for 3â€“4 minutes. Add coriander leaves and let it cool.
  4. Divide the dough into equal portions. Roll each into an oval and cut in half.
  5. Shape each half into a cone, fill with the potato mixture, and seal the edges with water.
  6. Deep fry in medium-hot oil until golden brown and crispy.
  7. Serve hot with mint chutney or tamarind sauce.
