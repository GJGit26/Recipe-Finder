Burger Recipe

Juicy, Flavorful Homemade Burgers

Ingredients

For the Patty:

  • 500g ground beef or chicken
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp paprika or chili powder (optional)

Other Ingredients:

  • Burger buns
  • Cheese slices
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Tomato slices
  • Onion rings
  • Pickles (optional)
  • Mayonnaise, ketchup, mustard

Instructions

  1. Mix ground meat with onion, garlic, egg, breadcrumbs, salt, pepper, and spices.
  2. Form into patties and refrigerate for 15–30 minutes to firm up.
  3. Heat oil in a pan or grill. Cook patties for 4–5 minutes per side or until fully cooked.
  4. Toast the burger buns lightly.
  5. Assemble burgers: Spread mayo or sauce, add lettuce, tomato, patty, cheese, onion, and top bun.
  6. Secure with a skewer if needed, and serve hot with fries or salad.
