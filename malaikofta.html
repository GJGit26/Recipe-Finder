Malai Kofta Recipe

Creamy and Delicious Indian Vegetable Koftas

Ingredients

For the Koftas:

  • 2 cups boiled and mashed potatoes
  • 1 cup grated paneer
  • 2 tbsp cornflour or all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup chopped nuts (cashews, almonds, raisins)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for deep frying

For the Gravy:

  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 cup fresh cream
  • Salt to taste
  • 1/2 cup milk (optional, for adjusting gravy consistency)

Instructions

  1. Mix mashed potatoes, grated paneer, cornflour, nuts, and salt to make kofta dough.
  2. Shape into small balls and deep fry in hot oil until golden brown. Drain and set aside.
  3. Heat oil or ghee in a pan, saut√© onions until golden.
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 1-2 minutes.
  5. Add tomato puree, turmeric, chili powder, salt, and cook until oil separates.
  6. Add garam masala and mix well.
  7. Add fresh cream and stir. Add milk if needed to adjust gravy consistency.
  8. Simmer for 5 minutes on low heat.
  9. Add fried koftas to the gravy just before serving.
  10. Garnish with cream or chopped coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or rice.
