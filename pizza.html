Pizza Recipe

Delicious Homemade Italian-Style Pizza

Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dry yeast
  • ¾ cup warm water

For the Toppings:

  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 ½ cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Vegetables (onion, bell pepper, mushrooms, etc.)
  • Pepperoni or other meats (optional)
  • Oregano and chili flakes

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it rest for 10 minutes until frothy.
  2. Add flour, salt, and olive oil. Mix and knead into a soft dough. Cover and let it rise for 1 hour.
  3. Preheat oven to 475°F (245°C).
  4. Roll out the dough on a floured surface. Transfer to a pizza pan or baking sheet.
  5. Spread pizza sauce, then add cheese and toppings of your choice.
  6. Bake for 12–15 minutes or until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  7. Remove, slice, sprinkle with oregano or chili flakes, and serve hot!
