Pasta Recipe

Simple and Delicious Italian Pasta

Ingredients

  • 200g pasta (penne, spaghetti, or your choice)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup tomato sauce or marinara sauce
  • 1/2 tsp chili flakes (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil leaves or dried Italian herbs
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Instructions

  1. Boil pasta in salted water according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan. Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add tomato sauce, chili flakes, salt, and pepper. Cook for 5–7 minutes on medium heat.
  4. Toss the cooked pasta into the sauce and mix well.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil or dried herbs and sprinkle with Parmesan cheese if desired.
  6. Serve hot with garlic bread or a fresh salad.
