Shahi Paneer Recipe

Rich and Creamy North Indian Delight

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer (cottage cheese), cubed
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1/4 cup cashew nuts, soaked and blended
  • 1/4 cup fresh cream
  • 2 tbsp ghee or oil
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

  1. Heat ghee in a pan and sauté cumin seeds, then add onions and cook until golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  3. Add tomato puree, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Cook until oil separates.
  4. Add cashew paste and cook for 2–3 minutes while stirring continuously.
  5. Add cream and mix well. Let it simmer for a minute.
  6. Add paneer cubes and garam masala. Cook for 5–6 minutes on low heat.
  7. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with naan or jeera rice.
